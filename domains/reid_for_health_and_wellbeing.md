# REID for Health & Well‑Being
### A structural guide for physical health, mental health, emotional regulation, burnout prevention, and long‑arc well‑being

Health is not a collection of habits.  
Health is a **structural system**.

Your physical, emotional, and cognitive well‑being all operate through
the same four invariants:
- State  
- Forces  
- Horizon  
- Flows  

REID provides a universal framework for understanding and improving
health across all domains of life.

This document explains how to apply REID to health and well‑being.

---

# 1. Why Health Needs REID

People struggle with:
- Burnout  
- Stress  
- Overwhelm  
- Emotional overload  
- Poor habits  
- Inconsistent routines  
- Conflicting priorities  
- Lack of direction  

REID solves these structurally by modeling:
- Your current condition (state)

Health becomes structural, not motivational.

# 2. Modeling Health with the Four Invariants

### **State**
Your current physical, emotional, and cognitive condition.

Examples:
- Fatigue  
- Stress  
- Pain  
- Clarity  
- Mood  
- Sleep quality  

### **Forces**
Pressures acting on your health.

Examples:
- Work stress  
- Family demands  
- Emotional load  
- Environmental factors  
- Internal expectations  

### **Horizon**
Your health goals and direction.

Examples:
- More energy  
- Better sleep  
- Reduced stress  
- Improved strength  
- Emotional stability  

### **Flows**
Resources that support health.

Examples:
- Time  
- Energy  
- Attention  
- Emotional capacity  
- Nutrition  
- Movement  
- Rest  

Health is a structural system.
