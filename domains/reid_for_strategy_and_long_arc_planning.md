# 2. Modeling Strategy with the Four Invariants

### **State**
Where you are now.

Examples:
- Organizational maturity  
- Financial position  
- Team capability  
- Emotional climate  
- Technical architecture  
- Market position  

### **Forces**
What is pushing on the system.

Examples:
- Competition  
- Deadlines  
- Regulations  
- Stress  
- Expectations  
- Opportunities  
- Constraints  

### **Horizon**
Where you are going.

Examples:
- 3‑year vision  
- 10‑year trajectory  
- Identity arc  
- Market position  
- Family direction  
- Organizational mission  

### **Flows**
What you have to work with.

Examples:
- Time  
- Money  
- Talent  
- Energy  
- Compute  
- Attention  
- Emotional capacity  

Strategy is invariant‑driven.
